SITES (Swedish Infrastructure for Ecosystem Science) is a national coordinated infrastructure for terrestrial and limnological field research. SITES is funded by the Swedish Research Council and the five responsible universities where Abisko Scientific Research Station, Erken Laboratory and the field research stations at Tarfala, Svartberget, Röbäcksdalen, Grimsö, Lönnstorp, Asa and Skogaryd participates. Swedish University of Agricultural Sciences is the coordinator.
